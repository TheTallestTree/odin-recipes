Peanut Butter Balls


Description

Healthy, not too sweet, a great snack for anytime! Perfect for those occasions when you just can't get enough of peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup bron rice syrup
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups oats, or more to taste
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened flaked coconut

Steps

  1. Combine brown rice syrup and coconut oil together in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until melted and boiling, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat.
  2. Mix peanut butter and vanilla extract into syrup mixture until smooth. Add oats, raisins, and coconut to peanut butter mixtrue and mix well. Refrigerate mixture until cool, at least 30 minutes. Form mixture into balls and freeze until set, at least 1 hour.
