Black Bean Soup


Description

This meatless, taco-flavored soup is loaded with bean protein and robust flavor from the combination of spices and fire roasted tomates. Pefect for game time or an evening watching Blues Clues, this one will sure be the treat you are dreaming of, if made correctly that is.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, schopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 1/4 cup frozen corn
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 medium jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained

Steps

  1. Heat oil ina Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, bell peppers, celery, carrots, jalapeno peppers, and garlic; cook until veggies soften, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add diced tomates; stir well and cook for 3 minutes. Add vegetable broth, black and pinto beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, paprika, and salt; stir until well combined. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
